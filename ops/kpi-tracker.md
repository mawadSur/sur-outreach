## Funnel math — backward from ~$30k/mo Year-1 goal

Target: 2 Fractional CTO retainers @ $5k + 1 AI project/mo @ $20k ≈ **2 net new deals/mo**.

Expected conversion (cold, targeted, personalized B2B — estimates, verify against your own data):

| Stage | Pessimistic | **Expected** | Optimistic |
|-------|------------|-------------|------------|
| Request → accepted | 20% | **30%** | 40% |
| Accepted → reply | 15% | **25%** | 35% |
| Reply → call booked | 20% | **30%** | 40% |
| Call → paid assessment | 25% | **30%** | 40% |
| Assessment → implementation/retainer | 40% | **50%** | 60% |
| **Cumulative request → meeting** | 0.6% | **2.2%** | 5.6% |

Required monthly volume (expected case) to net 2 deals:

| Metric | Required/mo |
|--------|-------------|
| Implementations won | 2 |
| Paid assessments (÷50%) | ~4 |
| Discovery calls (÷30%) | ~13 |
| Replies (÷30%) | ~44 |
| Accepts (÷25%) | ~178 |
| **Connection requests (÷30%)** | **~593/mo ≈ 140/wk ≈ 27/working-day** |

Capacity reality (pilot): ONE account safely sustains only ~**75 invites/wk ≈ ~320/mo** (the binding
~80/wk LinkedIn ceiling) — **BELOW the ~593/mo the expected case needs for 2 deals/mo.** So the `your-account`
pilot is a **conversion-validation run, not full capacity**: expect well under 2 deals/mo from it alone. Reach
volume by adding a second account (deferred UAE, or a second US) once the funnel rates are proven. Levers are
targeting quality + the paid-assessment gate, NOT pushing one account past ~80/wk (that just gets it restricted).
